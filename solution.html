H&ET

Help and Earn Together



H&ET is a mobile app, In which every indain is a worker and work provider. In this app no one is a permanent worker and work provider. You post your requirement and get the solution form other perosn. you work according to the requirement using your minimal skill sets for hours or a day in free time and earn money.

Another main feature of our app is fare sharing, where you find a fare sharer along with you in a journey


Features of our app

  • FInd instant worker
  • Do instant work and earn instantly
  • Location based work assignation
  • No permanent workers needed
  • Post your requiremnt (any type of worker you need)
  • Providing on demand workers(hourly or per day based working)
  • Reducing unemployment upto some extent, helping students and other employees to earn money in their free time
  • works or working sectors are not restricted. we can add categories of work, based on requirements(dynamically updating working requirements)
  • FARE SHARING (TO GET AWAY WITH PETROL HIKES)
  • IMPACTING LARGE NUMBER OF PEOPLE(Nearly 10MILLIONS(lower/middle class families))
